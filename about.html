I'm a second-year master's student at the University of Michigan studying user experience design and research. I have a background in understanding human behavior, having studied cognitive science as an undergrad. I love problem-solving and working with others!

I've been an avid tech fan since I was little, constantly reading about the newest tech products and tinkering with any software application that I found interesting. When Google released its first Material Design guidelines, I became enamored with design, and it became my mission to learn how to make attractive and usable designs!
